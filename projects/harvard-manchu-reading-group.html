Harvard Manchu Reading Group

Formed after a semester of virtual intermediate Manchu at Harvard University, the Harvard Manchu Reading Group meets virtually once every two weeks to read and discuss various Manchu texts, ranging from memorials to poetry to philosophy and anatomies.

Current members include:
Sarah Bramao-Ramos, Harvard University
Chao Lang, Harvard University
Preetam Prakash, Stanford University
Baoqing Qian, Boston University
Jack Isaac Rabinovitch, Harvard University
Chenyu Tu, Brown University
