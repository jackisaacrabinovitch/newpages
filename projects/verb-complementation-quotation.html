Projects

Verb Complementation Strategies and Quotation as a Complement

I have been researching the different ways that different languages embed under attitude predicates, and specifically where and when it is okay to embed quotations and demonstrations under attitude verbs. I have argued for (speech) acts as belonging to their own ontological sort, which allows embedding of intent or sincerity as separate from truth conditions.
